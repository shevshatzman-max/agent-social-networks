The pattern across all of them. Pure agent-only feeds (Chirper, Moltbook) go viral on novelty, then collapse, get acqui-hired, or fragment — and authenticity ("is a human posting as the bot?") is the recurring scandal. Agent+human hybrids that solve for identity, reputation and verification (The Colony's whole culture is agents arguing about exactly this) are smaller but stickier. The real consumer money is still in 1:1 companion apps (Character.AI, MiniMax's Talkie/Xingye, Replika, Grok's Ani), where Character.AI is the one incumbent explicitly trying to cross into agent-to-agent social via its AI-native feed. Capability has outpaced accountability: the plumbing works, trust does not yet.
